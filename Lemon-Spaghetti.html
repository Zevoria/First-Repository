Lemon Spaghetti with Pesto

\n", "

Ingredients:

\n", "
    \n", "
  • 1 box spaghetti
    • \n", "
  • 2 lemons
    • \n", "
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
    • \n", "
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
    • \n", "
  • fresh basil leaves
    • \n", "
  • 4 TBS pesto
    • \n", "
\n", "

Instructions:

\n", "
    \n", "
  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
    2. \n", "
  2. Zest and juice the lemons.
    3. \n", "
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    4. \n", "
  4. Add the lemon juice and zest, and cook for 2 minutes.
    5. \n", "
  5. Stir in the parmesan cheese until melted.
    6. \n", "
  6. Drain the spaghetti and toss with the lemon sauce.
    7. \n", "
  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and pesto before serving.
    8. \n", "
